Web development is the process of building and maintaining websites and web applications that function on the internet. It encompasses various tasks, including web design, web programming, and database management, to create interactive and functional online experiences.

Pizza is one of the most loved foods in the world, originating from Naples, Italy, where the famous Margherita pizza was created with tomato, mozzarella, and basil to represent the Italian flag. Over time, pizza has evolved into countless varieties—from thin-crust Neapolitan and cheesy deep-dish Chicago style to spicy Indian versions topped with paneer or tandoori chicken. What makes pizza special is its universal appeal: it’s customizable, perfect for sharing, and always comforting. Whether enjoyed at a party, a family dinner, or a quick snack, pizza continues to be more than just food—it’s a global symbol of joy and togetherness.
